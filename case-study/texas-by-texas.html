Government Web & Native App Product Design

Texas by Texas Web App & Native App

Designing a unified digital platform that gives Texans a single, streamlined way to access state services across web and mobile.

Role Product Designer
Sector Government
Platform Web & iOS/Android

The Challenge

Texans interacting with state government services faced a fragmented digital landscape. The vision for Texas by Texas (TxT) was to create a single, unified platform where residents could access all state services through one account.

  • Dozens of separate agency portals with different login systems and navigation
  • No unified identity — users managed multiple accounts across agencies
  • Inconsistent experiences between web and mobile touchpoints
  • High barrier to entry for residents unfamiliar with government systems
Challenge overview image

The Design Process

I worked closely with cross-functional teams across multiple state agencies to map the service ecosystem and define a scalable design approach that could accommodate a wide range of government services.

  • Mapped the full service ecosystem across agencies to identify highest-impact journeys
  • Designed a unified account system with single sign-on across all state services
  • Created a clean service dashboard surfacing personalized tasks and deadlines
  • Built consistent design patterns across web and native iOS/Android apps
  • Ensured seamless cross-platform context so users could switch devices without losing progress
Design process image

Impacts

The TxT platform expanded access to state services for millions of Texans, reducing friction and establishing a scalable foundation for future service integrations.

  • Unified access to state services through a single account across web and mobile
  • Reduced friction of navigating government systems for millions of residents
  • Established a scalable design system for onboarding new agency services
  • Improved accessibility and inclusivity across all digital touchpoints
